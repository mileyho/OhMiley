About Me

畢業於輔仁大學影像傳播學系--多媒體設計組；網頁設計工作者，擅長網頁設計、平面設計、電腦繪圖，包含網站架設、網頁排版、網頁廣告、EDM設計‧‧‧‧‧‧等。
I graduated from Fu-Jen Catholic University, majoring in Communication Art -- Multimedia Design. I'm a web designer, focused on web design, graphic design and illustration, including web layouts, banner, EDM and many others.

Skills

Photoshop, Illustrator / HTML, CSS, CSS3, RWD / JavaScript, jQuery
